Japan Design & Invention Expo (JDIE) 2020

International exams
Saturday, 4/18/2020 10:05:21 PM | GMT+7


1. About competition: The World Invention Intellectual Property Associations would like to invite all inventors to participate in the 2020 Japan Design, Idea and Invention Expo. It will be a three-day event for the exhibition from July 5 - 7, competition including the award ceremony. The World Invention Intellectual Property Associations and Chizal Corporation were organized this exhibition. A lot of people joined this year’s Japan Design, Idea and Invention Expo especially students, companies and Resource Persons. This is because the exhibition has no age limit and is open to professionals as well. They all come here to communicate with other inventors and companies.
One big difference of the Expo is the participants can present their works in any ways, to be more creative and innovative to get visitors, judges or buyers’ attention. More than 100 schools such as Tokyo University, Chiba University, Yokohama National University, Tokyo Denki University, Tokyo University of Agriculture, Tokai University and Musashino Academia Musicae will be participated in Japan Design & Invention Expo

2. Date: 5 - 7 July 2020

3. Venue: Hotel Kyoto Eminence, Kyoto, Japan

4. Organizer: The World Invention Intellectual Property Associations (WIIPA) Chizal Corporation
