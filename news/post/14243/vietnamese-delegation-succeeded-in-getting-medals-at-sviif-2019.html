Vietnamese delegation succeeded in getting medals at SVIIF 2019

News
Friday, 4/17/2020 3:43:13 PM | GMT+7
The Silicon Valley International Invention Festival (SVIIF) is held to announce cutting-edge inventions around the world and create opportunities for homeowners to not only exchanges, learns but also directly contact with businessmen, representatives of famous companies and industrial organizations in Santa Clara. The contest took place from June 24 to 26, 2019 at Santa Clara Convention Center - Silicon Valley, where located leading names in the technology world such as Facebook, Google, Apple, Adobe Systems, Advanced Micro Devices (AMD), eBay or Intel.
The event attracted more than 400 inventors from 30 countries with more than 500 inventions.
The Research Institute for Creative Education (RICE) is honored to be a prestigious scientific organization authorized by SVIIF to act as the exclusive representative in Vietnam.
For the first time participating in the competition, 3 teams excellently won 2 Special prizes, 2 Silver Medals and 1 Bronze Medal with the researches on biochemical applications in real life. This is the first time the Vietnamese delegation achieve Silver Medal Exam on an American science and technology competition.
 

Photos of students during the competition
Beside the competition, the Vietnamese delegation also had the opportunity to experience Silicon Valley hi-tech center, where the tech giants such as Apple, Facebook and Google are located. Students are able to visit the world's largest campus of Stanford University - a prestigious school that has trained many talents for Silicon Valley.
