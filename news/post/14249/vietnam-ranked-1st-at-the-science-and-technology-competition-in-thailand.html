Vietnam ranked 1st at the Science and Technology Competition in Thailand

News
Friday, 4/17/2020 5:01:56 PM | GMT+7
The Vietnamese Delegation led by Research Institute of Creative Education the 2020 Bangkok International Intellectual Property, Invention, Innovation and Technology Exposition - IPITEx), held annually as a of the Thailand Inventor Festival from February 2 to 6, at Bangkok International Trade and Exhibition Center (BITEC). Research Institute of Creative Education is honored to be the representative authorized by the National Research Council of Thailand (NRCT) to organize the selection of annual participating teams in Vietnam. IPITEx 2020 has more than 1500 inventions from many countries around the world such as Romania, Canada, England, Singapore, China ... competing together. Attending with 5 teams, Team RICE won 5 Gold Medals, 3 special prizes from the Indonesian Patent and Inventions Association INNOPA, 2 special prizes from Singapore. The overall results ranked 1st among all participating delegations. This is the best achievement of Vietnamese Delegation in IPITEx so far.
 

The inventions of Vietnamese students are highly appreciated at IPITEx 2020. Researches are new, practical and of high applications combined with impressive presentation ability of contestants received much praise in this international arena. This result is due to the hard work of the students as well as the guidance of the enthusiastic teachers and leaders of IPITEx Vietnam 2020.
 

IPITEx is a big competition organized and sponsored by the Thai Government. The program was officially opened and awarded to outstanding scientists and inventors by the Prime Minister (or Deputy Prime Minister).
