Commendation Ceremony for winners of the International Science and Technology Olympiads in 2019

News
Thursday, 4/30/2020 5:40:00 PM | GMT+7
On August 11, Research Institute for Creative Education Science (RICE) in collaboration with Institute of Skills Education and Creative Intelligence (ISECI) of the University of Economics and Business - VNU held a ceremony to commend the students who achieved good prizes at international science and technology olympiads. Attending this ceremony were Assoc. Prof. Dr. Nguyen Truc Le - Rector of University of Economics and Business, Vietnam National University, Hanoi, Assoc. Dr. Chu Dinh Thuy - Permanent Vice Chairman of Vietnam Physics Association, Dr. Do Nhu Binh - Military Medical Academy, professional advisor of Vietnam delegation.
 
Assoc. Dr. Chu Dinh Thuy - Permanent Vice Chairman of Vietnam Physics Association

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Nguyen Truc Le - Rector of University of Economics and Business, Vietnam National University, Hanoi

Dr. Do Nhu Binh - Military Medical Academy
Research Institute of Creative Education (RICE) is proud to lead groups of Vietnamese students attending International Invention and Innovation (INTARG) in Poland, in which the teams won 3 gold and 2 silver medals. Also, in Silicon Valley International Invention Festival (SVIIF) in the United States, the teams won 2 Special Prizes, 2 Silver Medals and 1 Bronze Medal; this is the first time a Vietnamese delegation participating in science and technology competition in the US and won a Silver Medal.
This year, in the time participating in the Global Leadership Link Program in Singapore, team RICE excellently won 1 first prize and 1 best individual award. In Korea, 8 excellent teams led by RICE participated in World Invention and Creativity Olympiad won 8 gold medals.
